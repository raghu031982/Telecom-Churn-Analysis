## **Telecom-Churn-Analysis**
Predict behavior to retain customers.

**Summary of Findings:** Exploratory analysis reveals that customers with an International Plan and those making 4 or more service calls are significantly more likely to churn. My baseline Decision Tree model currently achieves a **Recall of 78.57%**, allowing the business to identify nearly 4 out of 5 at-risk customers before they depart. Moving forward to Module 24, I will implement ensemble techniques to improve this identification rate.
